About Rwanda Air

Rwanda Air is the national flag carrier airline of Rwanda, headquartered in Kigali. We connect Rwanda to the world with our growing network of destinations across Africa, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia.

Our modern fleet, exceptional service, and commitment to sustainability make us the airline of choice for travelers seeking comfort, reliability, and an authentic Rwandan hospitality experience.

With Rwanda Air, you're not just flying - you're experiencing the heart of Africa.
